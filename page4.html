Project Description: Intelligent Fitness App

Sedentary lifestyles are the cause of premature death and illness. A regular fitness regime is beneficial. One of the problems associated with such a regime, is that it is hard to stick to.
Can we create an artificially intelligent weight training app, similar in simplicity to SL 5*5, that analyses your past workouts, suggests what resistance you need for your next workout. And also rewards you by unlocking badges, powerups etc, that can be shared on FB, Twitter.
Can we make this app even more compelling by linking it to an online competitive game – similar to online multiplayer 'card' games, where the powerups and badges you win while exercising are transferred to your character to improve its performance
As a stretch goal – use Watson Personality Insights to add performance bonuses (using Twitter feed as an input). Encouraging positive behaviour. And so you will need to create a compelling fitness app, as well as a compelling online game
